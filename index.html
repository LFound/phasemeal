Why it works

Your body runs on cycles. When meals sync with those cycles, stress drops, sleep improves and weight trends move the right way—without obsessive tracking.

Timing first

Train a predictable rhythm. Finish 2–3 hours before sleep to let recovery do its thing.

Gentle composition

What you eat supports the rhythm: protein + complex carbs earlier; lighter, calmer meals later.

Measurable calm

Weekly check-ins on energy, cravings, sleep and mood. Track change without gadgets.

How it works

1) Short questionnaire

Tell us your wake time, typical meal pattern, evening appetite and sleep quality. We map your rhythm type.

2) Personal plan

You get a 6-week, timing-first plan: your meal windows, daily cut-off, hydration cues and two simple composition tips.

3) Weekly check-in

Rate energy, mood, cravings and sleep (1–5). We adjust your windows if needed—no calorie counting.

4) Sustain

Once your rhythm holds, you can coast with light reminders or seasonal resets. The goal is independence.
